Mission

“If you can’t explain it to a 6-year-old, you don’t understand it yourself.”
-Albert Einstein

Holexp abstracts all software syntax so that learners use plain text in coding and build up their confidence, engagement and skills through our unique platform. Holexp is powered by AI and uses NLP (in Arabic or English) as input to create functional automation projects using its open source hardware kit.

With Holexp, coding has never been easier! Holexp simplifies what is considered difficult in coding and hardware design. Programming and electronics is now for all: youth and anyone who have missed out on programming or electronics. Write it in your own language and Holexp will take care of the rest.

About Us

Holexp

Holexp brings innovation in both the form and the content of teaching programming and electronics.

Different from traditional ways of learning, Holexp engages young minds early in their learning journey and sets them on a path of lifelong learning.

A community

It is an online platform that can easily scale up to formal and informal education circles through a rich suite of video tutorials and a chatbot for 24/7 support.

Holexp needs minimal computing resources with its lightweight interface.

Holexp uses one of the most cost efficient and reliable open-source hardware ecosystem for the STEM community to embrace.

Our team

The founding team of Holexp is an energetic group of AI expert, software, automation and management looking to grow.
